# CLI Market — Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

**Effective date:** May 20, 2026

---

## 1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy defines the boundaries of acceptable use of the CLI Market Service. Violation may result in immediate suspension or termination under the Terms of Service.

## 2. Prohibited Conduct

2.1 **Illegal Activity.** You may not use the Service for any activity that violates applicable laws or regulations in your jurisdiction.

2.2 **Retailer Terms Violation.** You may not use the Service to:
- Place orders that violate the terms of service of any VTEX retailer
- Engage in automated purchasing intended to manipulate retailer inventory or pricing
- Use the Service for inventory hoarding, scalping, or unfair trade practices

2.3 **Competing Services.** You may not:
- Use the Service to build, train, or operate a substantially similar product
- Scrape, mirror, or bulk-export Data for redistribution
- Reverse engineer the aggregation, normalization, or SKU-matching algorithms

2.4 **Abuse and Overload.** You may not:
- Exceed rate limits for your access tier
- Attempt to bypass authentication, authorization, or rate limiting
- Submit queries designed to degrade Service performance
- Use the Service in a manner that imposes unreasonable load on retailer APIs

2.5 **Harmful Content.** You may not use the Service to search for, compare, or purchase:
- Illegal goods or controlled substances
- Weapons or hazardous materials where prohibited by law
- Counterfeit goods or goods that infringe intellectual property rights

2.6 **Data Misuse.** You may not:
- Use commerce data to track or profile individuals without consent
- Combine CLI Market Data with other datasets to re-identify individuals
- Resell user-specific purchase data

## 3. Automated Agents

3.1 MCP tools and AI agents may use the Service provided they comply with this AUP.

3.2 Autonomous checkout (`market_checkout`) requires explicit human approval per transaction. Automated checkout without human review is prohibited.

3.3 Agent session identity must be maintained. Sharing agent credentials is prohibited.

## 4. Reporting Violations

Report suspected violations to: abuse@cli-market.dev

## 5. Enforcement

5.1 CLI Market may:
- Suspend or rate-limit accounts that violate this AUP
- Terminate accounts for serious or repeated violations
- Report illegal activity to law enforcement

5.2 CLI Market will provide notice of enforcement actions where commercially reasonable.
