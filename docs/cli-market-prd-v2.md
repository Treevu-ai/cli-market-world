# PRD: CLI Market v2.0 — From Beta to Growth
**Status**: Draft  
**Author**: Product Manager 🧭 (Agency Agent)  
**Last Updated**: 2026-05-28  
**Version**: 2.0  

## 1. Problem Statement
CLI Market v1.4.3 proves the thesis: AI agents need commerce infrastructure. But three gaps block growth:
1. **Retailer onboarding is human-dependent.** Every retailer requires a founder email or LinkedIn DM.
2. **No revenue loop.** Pro plan ($79/mo) has sandbox PayPal, not live. Zero revenue.
3. **Data moat is invisible.** 43K+ prices exist but the dashboard is internal, not a product.

## 2. Goals & Success Metrics
| Goal | Metric | Baseline | Target | Window |
|------|--------|----------|--------|--------|
| Retailer growth | Self-serve onboardings/week | 0 | 10 | 60 days |
| Revenue | Monthly recurring revenue | $0 | $500 | 90 days |
| Agent adoption | Active MCP connections | Unknown | 50 | 60 days |
| Collector reliability | Stores >80% success | 28/41 (68%) | 41/41 (100%) | 30 days |

## 3. Three Pillars
**Pillar 1: Self-Serve Retailer Onboarding** — form on cli-market.dev/retailers, auto-validation, instant API key
**Pillar 2: Live Monetization** — PayPal live, `market upgrade` CLI command, tier enforcement
**Pillar 3: Public Data Products** — weekly "CLI Market Price Pulse" PDF report

## 4. Launch Plan
| Phase | Date | Audience | Gate |
|-------|------|----------|------|
| Alpha | Jun 1 | Internal + 3 partners | PayPal live, self-serve form |
| Beta | Jun 8 | Waitlist (LinkedIn + HN) | 10 self-serve retailers |
| GA | Jun 15 | Public | 10 retailers/week, $500 MRR |
